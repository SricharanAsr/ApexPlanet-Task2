## 📈 Visual Insights

### 1. Revenue by Category
*   **Electronics** is the leading revenue driver, followed by **Home**.
*   **Groceries** and **Beauty** have higher volume but lower individual transaction values.

### 2. Customer Demographics
*   The majority of our high-value customers fall within the **35-44 age bracket**.
*   Marketing focus should be adjusted to capture more of the **18-24** segment which currently shows lower engagement.

### 3. Monthly Trends
*   Sales are relatively stable, but a slight dip is observed in mid-year.
*   Promotion cycles could be introduced to flatten seasonality.

## 🛠 Target Actions
1.  **Upsell in Beauty/Groceries**: Introduce bundled deals to increase the average transaction value in these low-cost categories.
2.  **Loyalty Program**: Target the top 5 customers with exclusive offers to ensure retention.
3.  **Regional Campaigns**: While not currently in data, adding location data would be the next step for geographical targeting.
